Welcome to Mak & Co.

Chartered Accountants deliver high quality, cost effective & on time services.

Mak & Co. is one of the Bangladesh’s highly professional service provider firm, provides audit, tax, enterprise risk services, outsourcing of accounting services, financial advisory and consulting to a wide range of local and multinational clients.

The firm was established in 2006 as a consultancy firm and it has become one of the leading professional services firms in Bangladesh and registered as a CA firm lately. The firm is providing services to over several clients including both multinational and local companies. The firm has significant presence across manufacturing, services, commercial and other services in the country.

The firm has a team of efficient workforce in their registered office located at Motijheel and two other support office located at Banani and DOHS Mirpur. Our staff includes qualified and part qualified accountants, MBAs, IT experts and other technically qualified persons.

Mak & Co. brings together clients, offers innovative solutions, and endeavors to exceed client expectations. The firm adopts a customized, responsive and personal approach towards client service and offers a complete range of services across various industries.
