Meet Our Team

Mr Md. Ali Kawsar, ACA earned his Associate Chartered Accountant membership and practicing license from Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh. Mr. kawsar also obtained both his BBA & MBA degree in Accounting & Information Systems from the reputed University of Dhaka. He also earned Certificate in Supply Chain Management degree from International Trade Center. He has diversified working experience in oil &Gas Company, telecommunication, information technology and software company, consumer products, audit and consultancy firm etc at home & abroad.

Mr. Kawsar has developed expertise with regard to forming of various companies and setting up of organizational structure such as accounting system, HR system etc. He has experience in accounting record preparation & maintenance, financial control & management effectively and efficiently, Compliance of IFRS, tax, VAT, companies act, company rules & regulations etc. He also has expertise in Valuation & Due Diligence, Tax Consultancy, Special Audit, Sarbanes Oxley (SOX) Audit, financial audit, IPO documents preparations & compliance, complete solution of IPO issue & approval augmentation etc.Mr. Kawsar audited and worked with some of the largest private and publicly traded companies in the world.

Mr. Kawsar is the member/life member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh (ICAB), Dhaka Taxes Bar Association (DTBA), Supply Chain Management Professionals of International Trade Center (ITC),Review Committee for Published Accounts & Reports (Annual Report) of ICAB, and Dhaka University Alumni Association (DUAA).

Albab Al Mehboob, CPA earned his Certified Public Accountant membership and license from The Institute of Certified Public Accountants, Ireland, UK and permitted to practice professionally by the government of Her Majesty's Government of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. He is a professional Financial & Cost Accountant and earned his Associate membership as a Financial Accountant from The Institute of Financial Accountants, UK and Certificate in Cost Accountancy from London Chamber of Commerce Industiries, UK. Mr. Albab also earned his Hons from one of the leading business school of the world named Edinburgh Business School, (HWU, UK).

Mr. Albab audited and worked with some of the largest publicly traded companies in the world. In addition, he has been the Executive Director of DIFT BML and Chief Financial Officer at Ethics Development Ltd. He also managed smaller regional CA firms that introduced him to many small businesses.

Mr. Albab has lectured and taught several accounting, auditing, business planning, record keeping classes at Bangladeshi and UK universities in the past. He is an active member of the RAOWA Club and Lifetime member at Herriot Watt Alumni Association.

Apart from being CPA, he has obtained Business Sustainability Programme from the - Institute of Chartered Accountants of England & Wales (ICAEW, UK). He has been recently also awarded membership to Institute of Public Accountants (Australia). Mr. Zaman completed his grad with distinctions from one of the leading business school named Edinburgh Business School, (HWU, UK).
